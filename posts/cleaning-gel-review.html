We Tested The Viral Cleaning Gel That's Everywhere

You've seen it in those hypnotizingly satisfying videos. A bright, colorful slime being pressed into a dusty car vent or a crumb-filled keyboard, and peeling back perfectly clean, leaving no residue. It looks like magic. But does it actually work?

We ordered the best-selling **Dust Cleaning Gel** on AliExpress to find out if it lives up to its viral reputation. After a week of pressing it into every dirty crevice we could find, here's our honest verdict.

What Exactly Is It?

This cleaning gel is a biodegradable, non-sticky, putty-like substance. Think of it as a high-tech version of the slime you played with as a kid. It's designed to conform to any shape, grabbing dust and debris from tight spaces where cloths and brushes can't reach.

The Good, The Great, and The "Genius"

  • It's Incredibly Satisfying: The main selling point is the experience. Pressing the cool, pliable gel into a dirty spot and seeing it come away clean is genuinely fun. It turns a boring chore into a satisfying activity.
  • Perfect for Cars and Keyboards: This is where it truly shines. It effortlessly cleans air vents, cup holders, gear shifts, and the impossible-to-clean spaces between keyboard keys.
  • Reusable and Simple: You can use it over and over again until it changes to a dark color, which indicates it's full of dirt. No washing, no mess, just put it back in its jar.
  • Surprisingly Effective: We were skeptical, but it picked up an impressive amount of dust, crumbs, and lint without leaving any sticky residue behind.

The Verdict: A Fun and Functional Find

Is this a deep-cleaning tool that will replace all your brushes and vacuums? No. But is it a brilliantly effective, fun, and cheap solution for cleaning all the awkward nooks and crannies in your life? Absolutely.

For its low price, the cleaning gel is a fantastic and surprisingly useful tool to have on hand. It's a certified "genius" find for anyone who loves a clean car or a crumb-free desk.
